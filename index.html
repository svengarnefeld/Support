Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection & Processing: Vibe Deck collects voice input (via microphone) and images (via camera or photo library). We only collect this data after obtaining your explicit in-app consent.

2. Data Usage: The collected voice recordings, text prompts, and images are used solely for the purpose of generating code and UI designs within the app.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing: To provide its AI functionalities, this app transmits your voice input, images, and text prompts to a third-party AI service provider (OpenAI). We confirm that OpenAI provides the same or equal protection of user data as stated in this privacy policy. No personal data is stored on our own servers.

4. Local Storage: Your project history, generated code, and API keys are stored securely and locally on your device.

5. Payments: All transactions are handled securely by Apple via In-App Purchases.
